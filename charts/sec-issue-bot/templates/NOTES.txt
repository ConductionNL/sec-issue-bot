The Security Incident bot runs in Slack Socket Mode and does not expose a Service.

To view logs:
  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} logs deploy/{{ include "sec-issue-bot.fullname" . }}

Ensure the referenced Secret exists with required keys:
  - OPENAI_API_KEY
  - SLACK_BOT_TOKEN
  - SLACK_APP_TOKEN
Optional Jira keys:
  - JIRA_URL
  - JIRA_EMAIL
  - JIRA_API_TOKEN
  - JIRA_PROJECT_KEY
  - JIRA_ISSUE_TYPE
